Haresh Karnan is a PhD student at The University of Texas at Austin where he works on
Reinforcement Learning, AI and Machine Learning for Robotics. He received his MS in Aerospace
Engineering from Texas A&M University in College Station, Texas in 2018, where he was advised
by Robert Skelton, and he received a Bachelors in Technology in Instrumentation and Control
Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli in 2016.
